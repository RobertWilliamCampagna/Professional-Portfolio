About
Me

Robert William Campagna. born on Long Island, NY. I will tell you all a story, how having surprise twins can save your life. I was unprepared. For the twins yes, but also for what happened to me six months after they were born. I needed a second job, and by not getting a job at my favorite donut shop for the overnight shift, I had no prior experience in making yeast rise, I decided with the extra time I will start playing baseball again after a ten year break. Bad idea. My third game back I was pitching and I broke my arm in half longways, think about that. It was like taking a piece of celery and twisting it, thats how my break was. I broke my humerus while throwing a fastball...felt like my arm got shot off my a bazooka. Anyway, I had to tell my job that I hurt myself playing baseball. Thank God my boss was an ex major league baseball player so he could undertand the pain I was going through. I had to leave my job of being Optimus Prime at Universal Studios for a hard nine month recovery. I had surgery, the doctor put a metal bar and ten screws in my arm, and oh boy it feels like it. During my rehab I saw a bunch of doctors that didn't like the fact my arm broke throwing a baseball so they sent me out for many tests. Turns out I had thyriod cancer and I needed half my thyroid removed. Took care of that real quick, got my job back as Optimus, but guess what? Still not enough money to support a family, so thats why I am here trying to get a new wanted skill so I can go out and get a good job for my family :) thats it...this was like therapy. Now enjoy the picture of a light house on the very eastern tip of Long Island, and two Mr. Potato heads that one of my sons Welsey built.
